About us

Established in 1986, Flower Power emerged as Egypt’s
first floral design house renowned for its flowers and creative displays.
With over 30 years of market
expertise and a full-fledged team working across Egypt, the one-stop design house offers
styling for weddings, themed events and corporate functions as well as visual merchandise and artistic flowers for prestigious hotels and fashion brands.
